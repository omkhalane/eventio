# Code Of Conduct

Eventio is committed to being a respectful, constructive, and welcoming open-source project.

## Our Standards

Behavior that helps the project:

- Be clear, kind, and direct.
- Give feedback about the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent while still asking precise questions.
- Respect different experience levels and communication styles.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on improving Eventio.
- Credit ideas, source research, and implementation help where appropriate.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Spam, trolling, or bad-faith participation.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors to disclose private information.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation.
