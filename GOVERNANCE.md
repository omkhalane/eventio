# Governance

Eventio is currently maintained by [Om Khalane](https://github.com/omkhalane).

## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Keep the project usable and trustworthy.
- Review issues and pull requests.
- Protect user privacy and source-platform terms.
- Maintain clear architecture and contribution standards.
- Prioritize stability over novelty.

## Decision Making

The maintainer makes final decisions on scope, architecture, roadmap, and
releases. Contributors are encouraged to propose changes through focused issues
and pull requests.

## Project Values

- Clear ownership.
- Practical architecture.
- Respectful collaboration.
- Reliable data handling.
- Contributor-friendly documentation.
